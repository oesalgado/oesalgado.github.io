So WTF is this tool and why was it created?

2019.3.24

This calculator is primarily made for young adults to help them assess whether or not what they're about to buy is really a good decision.

But why a calculator that tells me how many Chick-Fil-A sandwhiches I can purchase?

Well to be quite honest this is a system that I've used to keep myself from making dumb financial decisions like purchasing something over $100 that will bring me very little utitlity.

I enjoy Chick-Fil-A a lot so everytime I think about making a relatively dumb purchase especially on depreciating assets I ask myself: "How many times can I go to Chick-Fil-A if I don't buy this?"

So that's it? It only tells me how many sandwhiches I can consume instead of my stupid impulse buy?

Well yes... but actually, no. At it's current state you're only able to calculate a sandwhich count but in the backend we're developing financial resources to make finance-management easy without all the confusing headaches.

Yes it sounds vague right now, but it will make your life easier."
